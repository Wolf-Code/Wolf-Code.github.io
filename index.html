I'm a 21 year old student at the Utrecht University, studying Game Technology. As much as I like both front-end and back-end developing, I prefer working with back-end as making something functional is right up my alley, making something look gorgeous, however, is not.

Latest Projects

Damage Control

The result of my graduation project: a serious game built inside a 3D Amsterdam, which has been reconstructed from a point-cloud. This project was made by a group of 12 people (10 programmers, 2 artists) at Utrecht University, on behalf of CycloMedia.

CycloMedia had gathered point cloud data from Amsterdam using their specialised cars and airplanes, which was then turned into vertices, indices and texture data. Said data was provided to us, and we used it to build a serious game in Unity3D.
